Builder Ballery - Toilet Layout Technical Checklist
Updated: 2026-02-24

Purpose:
Prevents common rework issues in toilet design and execution.

Site checklist:
1. Commode centerline and wall clearances confirmed before plumbing chase.
2. Health faucet placement verified for comfortable use.
3. Wash basin height and mirror alignment checked.
4. Floor slope tested to avoid water stagnation.
5. Trap locations and vent planning reviewed.
6. Waterproofing coat, curing period, and flood test completed.
7. Ventilation path available (window/exhaust).
8. Final fixture alignment checked before tile closeout.

Practical note:
Toilet mistakes are expensive to correct after finishing. Validate dimensions before concealed works.
